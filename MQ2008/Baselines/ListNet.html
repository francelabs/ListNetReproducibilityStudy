Introduction to ListNet
ListNet is a listwise ranking algorithm. Unlike pairwise approaches such as Ranking SVM and RankBoost using pairs of objects in learning, lists of objects are used as "instances" in listwise approch. ListNet employs cross entropy loss as the listwise loss function in gradient descent.

The details of ListNet can be found from this ICML paper.
